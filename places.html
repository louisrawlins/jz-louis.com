Places

Hotels

Rose Garden Inn
Cozy, small cottages in Berkeley. Louis's family enjoyed staying here last visit.
Hotel Shattuck Plaza
A solid, fancy place to stay in downtown Berkeley. Friends of Louis stayed here on a recent visit.
Bancroft Hotel
Recommended by UC Botanical Garden.
Stay at an Airbnb in Berkeley, CA
Jenny and Louis will be staying at a nearby Airbnb. If you need an Airbnb account, you can get a $55 discount with Jenny’s referral link.

Museums

San Francisco Museum of Modern Art (SFMOMA)
Jenny and Louis have a membership at SFMOMA and can get up to 4 tickets for free, daily. Please contact them if you would like to reserve tickets for a particular day and time. SFMOMA is closed on Wednedays.
Oakland Museum of California (OMCA)
Wonderful, California-local art and exhibitions near Lake Merritt in downtown Oakland.

Cafes and Bakeries

Third Culture Bakery
Mochi Muffins! Visit Sam and Wenter, friends of Jenny and Louis, and owners of this lovely bakery.
Blue Willow Teaspot
Laid-back place where you and relax and learn about new teas. Be mindful parking in this industrial area.
Wrecking Ball Coffee Roasters (North Berkeley)
Esspresso done right.
Blue Bottle
Jenny recommends their Oatly Latte and Beligan Waffle. They are headquartered in Oakland with locations throughout California.
Fournée Bakery
Louis recommends the Noisette cookie. Go early, because they sell out by the afternoon. Near the Claremont Hotel and Berkeley Tennis Club.
Highwire Coffee
Good coffee in the East Bay.
Trouble Coffee (West Oakland)
live fast, die old. Building community with coffee, toast and coconuts since 2007.

Restaurants

Saul’s Restaurant and Deli
Jewish soul food in Berkeley. Go here. Get the pickle plate, latkes and chicken matzo ball soup.
Rick and Ann’s
Good food, great staff. A couple doors down from Fournée Bakery in Berkeley.
Funky Elephant
Thai Fusion in Berkeley. Louis's favorite is the Kao Mun Gai (poached chicken breast with rice).
La Note
Delicious French food in Berkeley. Jenny and Louis wanted to take you here for brunch, but the reservation didn't work out. Visit anyway.
Marugame Udon
Udon on-the-go! Tasty noodles and croquettes in Berkeley. Worth it for the experience.
Ippudo Berkeley
Fancy ramen in Berkeley. Louis recommends having your noodles al dente.
Standard Fare
California fare in Berkeley. Next door to Third Culture Bakery. Get the salad dressing on the side.
B-Dama (inside Swan Market)
Japanese food in Downtown Oakland. Jenny recommends the vegetable tempura. Louis recommends the donburi bowls. Generous portions.
Brown Sugar Kitchen
Chicken and waffles, grits and all the goodies. Soul food in Downtown Oakland (and the Ferry Building in San Francisco).
Classic Guilin Rice Noodles
Rice Noodles in Oakland Chinatown. Family restaurant serving quality noodles from southeastern China.
Cookiebar Creamery
Excellent ice cream in Downtown Oakland. Jenny recommends Black Seasame and Ube flavors. Louis would suggest peanut butter pretzel, but they stopped making it. A couple blocks away from B-Dama.
Soba Ichi
Soba in West Oakland. Get there early, before they sell out.
